About Me:

I’m Kit, a game designer that specialises in UI/UX

Currently a master’s student at the University of Staffordshire; I focus on user-centred design and accessibility. The interface is the voice of the developer and if it is not clear then it is not heard, so I create this clarity with iterations based on the feedback of others as my perspective is not all seeing and not without its blind spots.

Besides designing games; I’m an avid tabletop fan whether that’s wargaming or role playing games, I also partake in a few outdoor hobbies such as hiking to try and mitigate all of the time my face is pressed against a screen.
